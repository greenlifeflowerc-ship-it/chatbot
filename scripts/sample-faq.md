## Shipping and delivery

We ship within 2 business days of an order being placed. Standard delivery takes
3 to 5 business days. Express delivery takes 1 to 2 business days. We currently
ship within the country only; international shipping is not available yet.

## Returns and refunds

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery, unworn and with tags attached.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days of
us receiving the returned item. Sale items are final and cannot be returned.

## Order changes and cancellations

An order can be changed or cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, while it is
still unfulfilled. After that the order has usually entered fulfilment and can no
longer be modified; in that case, wait for delivery and start a return.

## Sizing

Our sizing runs true to standard measurements. If you are between sizes we
recommend sizing up. A detailed size chart is linked on every product page.

## Contacting a person

If a customer wants to speak to a human, or raises a complaint, or asks anything
outside the topics above, the assistant should hand the conversation to a human
agent rather than guess.
